Meet our club Stanique Team

With years of experience in the sports industry, our team at Stanique Sports Management possesses a deep understanding of the intricacies involved in athlete representation, brand building, and contract negotiations.

We work closely with athletes from various disciplines, including but not limited to soccer, basketball, tennis, and more, to ensure their needs and aspirations are met at every step of their journey.

Our commitment

We develop student-athletes who are committed to personal excellence and team success in any athletic arenas and on the football field. We provide inspirational and motivational environments where preparation, practice and hard work generate legitimate opportunities for success.

Our team

Meet our highly trained and experienced team.

Stanley Emeka Mamah

CEO

With a deep-rooted love for sports, Stanley's journey in the sports management industry is driven by his unwavering dedication to empower athletes and redefine the standards of excellence in the field. His commitment to nurturing talent, providing personalized attention, and fostering strong relationships is at the heart of Stanique Sports Management's mission. Under Stanley's leadership, the agency aims to revolutionize the sports management landscape and guide athletes to greatness, both on and off the field.

William Iwedi

Team coach and manager of Stanique Football Club and Stanique Sports Management.

William Iwedi is a dynamic and passionate football coach who lives and breathes the sport. With an unwavering love for the game, he brings an infectious enthusiasm and a wealth of knowledge to his coaching role. Known for his ability to inspire and motivate players, William’s coaching style is a perfect blend of technical expertise and emotional connection. Background: William’s journey in football began at a young age, where he quickly developed a deep passion for the game. Growing up, he played for his local youth team and represented his school at various levels. His playing career laid a solid foundation for his transition into coaching, as he realized his true calling was in helping others excel on the pitch.
